ognl.MemberAccess
public class WhiteListMemberAccess
extends ognl.DefaultMemberAccess

Prevents access to static and instance members of all classes not in the white list (MemberAccessWhiteList.plist). This was done to prevent attacks such as @java.lang.System@exit(1) in the expression of a calculated column
